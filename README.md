# Habit Kingdom

Habit Kingdom is a fantasy nutrition and habit tracker for people who want to turn daily health routines into a small kingdom-building game.

## What it does

- Tracks meals, calories, protein, fat, carbs, fiber, and sugar.
- Lets users log water, sleep, and walking distance.
- Includes a built-in food database, custom foods, and a recipe calculator.
- Shows diary history in a calendar.
- Gives quests and advisor notes based on recent habits.
- Rewards progress with resources that can be used to unlock land and upgrade a medieval kingdom.
- Saves progress in the browser so the app can be used without an account server.

## Live link

https://your-username.github.io/habit-kingdom/

## How to use it

1. Open the live link and sign in or register with any demo email and password.
2. Choose the prepared demo version or start from zero.
3. Add your meals, water, sleep, and walking for the day, then collect resources and build your kingdom.

## Known limitations

- Registration is only a demo flow right now. There is no backend, database, or real user authentication yet.
- All user data is stored locally in the browser with `localStorage`. If the browser cache/site data is cleared, progress can be lost.
- The same account is not synced between different devices or browsers.
- Users currently enter their profile, nutrition targets, and habit goals manually.
- A future version could add an onboarding questionnaire to choose a goal, such as weight loss, maintenance, or muscle gain, and automatically calculate calorie and macro targets.
- The food database is limited and may not include every product or brand a user needs.
- Nutrition and habit insights are simple app guidance, not medical or professional diet advice.
